## Refund Policy

Last updated: {{January, 16, 2026}}

This Refund Policy explains the conditions under which refunds may be issued.

---

## 1. Digital Products & Services
All purchases made on this platform are **digital products or services**.  
Once a purchase has been completed and the product or service has been delivered, **refunds are generally not provided**.

---

## 2. Eligible Refund Cases
Refunds may be considered only under the following conditions:

- The product or service was **not delivered** as described
- A **technical error** caused duplicate charges
- The service was **unavailable for an extended period** due to our fault

Each request is reviewed **case by case**.

---

## 3. Non-Refundable Situations
Refunds will **not** be issued in the following cases:

- Change of mind after purchase
- Violation of rules or Terms of Service
- Account suspension or ban due to misconduct
- Partial use of services or subscriptions
- Purchases made by mistake

---

## 4. Chargebacks
Unauthorized chargebacks or payment disputes may result in:
- Immediate account suspension
- Permanent loss of access to services

Please contact support before initiating a chargeback.

---

## 5. How to Request a Refund
To request a refund, please contact our support team with:
- Proof of purchase
- Reason for the request
- Relevant transaction details

All requests must be submitted within **7 days** of purchase.

---

## 6. Policy Changes
We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time.  
Changes will be effective immediately upon posting.

---

## 7. Contact
If you have questions regarding this Refund Policy, please contact our support team.
