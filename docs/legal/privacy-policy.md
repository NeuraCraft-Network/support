## Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 18 January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect player data when using our Minecraft server and related services.  
By accessing or playing on our server, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect
We only collect data that is **necessary for server operation**, including but not limited to:

- Minecraft username
- Minecraft UUID
- IP address (for security and abuse prevention)
- Chat logs (for moderation purposes)
- Gameplay activity data
- Purchase and transaction data (if applicable)
- Discord ID (if accounts are linked)

We **do not collect sensitive personal data**, such as:
- Real names
- Home addresses
- Government identification numbers
- Payment card details (handled by third-party providers)

## 2. Minecraft Skin Usage
Our server and related services may access and use **Minecraft player skins**, including:

- Displaying skins in-game
- Rendering player avatars, heads, or profiles
- Showing skins on websites, leaderboards, or documentation pages

Skins are:
- Retrieved from Mojang or trusted third-party services
- Used only for visual and functional purposes
- Not modified or sold as standalone assets

All Minecraft skins remain the property of their respective owners and Mojang Studios.
