## Chat Rules

**Last updated:** 16 January 2026

These chat rules apply to **all communication channels** related to the server, including:
- In-game chat
- Private messages (if reported)
- Global chat
- Any server-related communication platforms (if applicable)

By using the chat, you agree to follow the rules below.

---

## 1. Be Respectful
- Treat all players and staff with respect
- No harassment, bullying, or personal attacks
- Discrimination based on race, gender, religion, nationality, or any personal attribute is not allowed

---

## 2. No Hate Speech or Extreme Content
The following are **strictly prohibited**:
- Hate speech
- Death threats
- Encouraging self-harm
- Excessive toxicity

This server aims to remain **safe and friendly for all ages**.

---

## 3. Language Usage
- Keep language appropriate and family-friendly
- Excessive swearing is discouraged
- Avoid bypassing chat filters using symbols or spacing

Mild language may be tolerated depending on context, but staff have final discretion.

---

## 4. Spamming & Flooding
- No spamming messages, characters, or emojis
- No excessive use of caps lock
- No repeated messages or copy-paste flooding

This includes advertising or repeating commands in public chat.

---

## 5. Advertising & Promotion
- Advertising other servers, websites, or social media is **not allowed**
- Sharing suspicious or malicious links is strictly prohibited

Server-approved links may be shared if permitted by staff.

---

## 6. Scams & Impersonation
- Impersonating staff or other players is not allowed
- Scamming or misleading players through chat is prohibited
- Fake giveaways or false announcements are not allowed

---

## 7. Staff Interaction
- Follow staff instructions at all times
- Do not argue excessively in public chat
- If you disagree with a decision, use proper appeal channels

Abuse or disrespect toward staff may result in stricter punishment.

---

## 8. Private Messages
- Harassment through private messages is not allowed
- Players may report private messages with valid proof
- Rules still apply even outside public chat

---

## 9. Sensitive Topics
The following topics should be avoided:
- Politics
- Real-world conflicts
- Controversial or inflammatory discussions

These topics often lead to unnecessary conflict.

---

## 10. Punishments
Violations of chat rules may result in:
- Warnings
- Temporary mutes
- Permanent mutes
- Temporary bans
- Permanent bans (for severe cases)

Punishments depend on **severity, repetition, and intent**.

---

## 11. Staff Discretion
Staff reserve the right to:
- Moderate chat as necessary
- Remove messages that disrupt the community
- Issue punishments without prior warning in extreme cases

---

By participating in chat, you agree to comply with all chat rules listed above.
