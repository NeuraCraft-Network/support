## Gameplay Rules

**Last updated:** 16 January 2026

These gameplay rules are designed to ensure **fairness, balance, and an enjoyable experience** for all players.  
By playing on this server, you agree to follow the rules below.

---

## 1. Fair Play
Players are **strictly prohibited** from using:
- Cheats, hacks, or unauthorized client modifications
- X-ray, auto-clickers, kill aura, fly hacks, or similar tools
- Any software that provides unfair gameplay advantages

Gameplay must rely on **vanilla Minecraft mechanics** and **server-provided features only**.

---

## 2. Bugs, Glitches, and Exploits
- Abusing bugs, glitches, or exploits for personal gain is not allowed
- This includes item duplication, economy exploits, or system bypasses
- Intentionally hiding bugs from staff is also considered a violation

If you discover a bug:
 **Report it to staff instead of abusing it.**

---

## 3. PvP & Combat
- PvP is only allowed in modes or areas where PvP is enabled
- Spawn killing and unfair combat abuse are not allowed
- Combat logging (disconnecting during combat) is prohibited

---

## 4. Griefing & Traps
- **Griefing and traps are only allowed in specific game modes or areas where they are explicitly permitted**
- Griefing or trapping players outside of allowed areas is prohibited
- Traps must not exploit bugs or cause excessive server lag

If a mode or area does not clearly allow griefing or traps, they are considered **not allowed**.

---

## 5. Flying & Movement
- Flying is **only permitted** through official **server-provided features**, such as:
  - Premium ranks
  - Server abilities
  - Game mode mechanics

- Using fly via **hack clients, modified launchers, or third-party software** is strictly prohibited

Unauthorized flying will be treated as cheating.

---

## 6. Farming, AFK, and Automation
- AFK is allowed as long as it does not negatively impact server performance
- Automatic farms are allowed **if they do not cause lag**
- Abuse of game mechanics that harms server stability is not allowed

Staff may disable or remove farms deemed excessive.

---

## 7. Economy & Trading
- Scamming or fraudulent trading is not allowed
- Manipulating the server economy is prohibited
- Real-money trading outside the official server store is not allowed

All trading must follow the serverâ€™s economy systems.

---

## 8. Illegal Items & Abuse
Players may not own or use:
- Items obtained through cheats or exploits
- Items with impossible or illegal enchantments
- Duplicated items

Illegal items may be **removed without notice**.

---

## 9. World & Environment
- Excessive world damage without purpose is discouraged
- Clean up abandoned structures when possible
- TNT, lava, and redstone must be used responsibly

---

## 10. Server Systems & Features
- Abuse of commands, permissions, or server systems is not allowed
- Exploiting premium features or event systems is prohibited
- Bugs affecting premium features are also considered exploits

---

## 11. Resets, Updates, and Changes
- Worlds or systems may be reset during major updates
- Gameplay mechanics may change over time
- Item or progress loss caused by official resets is not refundable

---

## 12. Enforcement & Punishments
Violations of gameplay rules may result in:
- Warnings
- Item removal
- Temporary bans
- Permanent bans
- Data resets

Punishments are based on **severity and intent**.

---

## 13. Staff Authority
Staff decisions are considered **final**.  
Abuse of appeal systems or excessive arguments may result in additional penalties.

---

By playing on this server, you acknowledge that you have read and agreed to these gameplay rules.
