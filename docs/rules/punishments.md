## Punishment System

**Last updated:** 16 January 2026

This document explains the types of violations and punishments that may be applied to players who break server rules.  
All punishments exist to maintain **fairness, safety, and community well-being**.

---

## 1. General Principles
- Punishments are issued based on **severity and intent**
- Repeated violations may result in escalated punishment
- Not all violations require a prior warning
- Staff decisions are considered **final**, subject to appeal where applicable

---

## 2. Types of Punishments
The following punishments may be applied:

- **Warning**  
  A verbal or written notice for minor violations

- **Temporary Mute**  
  Restricted chat access for a limited time

- **Permanent Mute**  
  Permanent restriction from using chat

- **Kick**  
  Temporary removal from the server

- **Temporary Ban**  
  Restricted access for a fixed duration

- **Permanent Ban**  
  Permanent removal from the server

- **Data / Item Reset**  
  Removal of items, progress, or player data

---

## 3. Minor Violations
Examples include:
- Minor chat spam
- Inappropriate language
- Mild disruption to gameplay
- Accidental rule violations

Typical punishments:
- Warning
- Short mute

---

## 4. Medium Violations
Examples include:
- Repeated spam
- Harassment or targeted toxicity
- Abuse of server features
- Minor scamming attempts

Typical punishments:
- Longer mute
- Kick
- Temporary ban

---

## 5. Severe Violations
Examples include:
- Cheating, hacking, or exploiting
- Griefing in prohibited areas
- Item duplication
- Hate speech or serious harassment
- Intentional abuse of bugs or glitches

Typical punishments:
- Long temporary ban
- Permanent ban
- Data reset without compensation

---

- Sexual harassment or sexual misconduct

---

## 7. Alternate Accounts (Alt Accounts)
- Using alternate accounts to evade punishment is prohibited
- Alt accounts may be punished accordingly
- IP bans may be applied when necessary

---

## 8. Appeals & Disputes
- Appeals must be submitted through official server channels only
- Appeals must include clear and honest explanations
- Abuse of the appeal system may result in denial of future appeals

Appeals do **not guarantee punishment removal**.

---

## 9. Purchased Benefits & Punishments
- Purchased perks may be revoked if used in violation of rules
- Punishments related to rule violations are **non-refundable**
- Contributions or purchases do not grant immunity

---

## 10. Changes to This System
We reserve the right to modify this punishment system at any time to maintain server integrity.

Changes take effect immediately after publication.
