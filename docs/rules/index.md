## Server Rules Overview

These rules apply to **all players** on the server.  
By playing on this server, you agree to follow all rules listed below and in related pages.

---

## Core Rules
- Respect all players and staff
- No cheating, hacking, or exploiting
- No harassment, hate speech, or excessive toxicity
- Follow staff instructions at all times

## Enforcement
Breaking the rules may result in warnings, mutes, kicks, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on severity.

Staff decisions are final.
